About me

Hi, my name is Nathan! I've been designing and developing games from a young age, and now I'm persuing my passion as a Game Design student at HKU in the Netherlands.

I started my journey trying to create experiences akin to my favourite games at the time, but since then my interest has veered away from persuing pure entertainment, in favor of more experimental and unconventional games.

For me, one of the most rewarding feelings is surprising players with how fun and refreshing experimental games can be. Such was the case with my 2025 end-year project Virtual Encounters.

Though this is not a hard rule I've set for myself; I enjoy creating more conventional games too, such as movement-based space shooter Thrust Factor.
